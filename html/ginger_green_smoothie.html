GINGER GREEN SMOOTHIE

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 cups chopped kale washed
  • 1 ripe medium banana, peeled
  • 1 small naval orange peeled
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened raw almond butter
  • 1 inch piece ginger, peeled and roughly chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soaked chia seed
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut water chilled
  • 1 cup ice
  • Pinch of kosher salt

INSTRUCTIONS:

To a blender add the kale, banana, orange, almond butter, ginger, chia seeds,
coconut water, ice and pinch of salt. Blend on high speed for 1 minute or until smooth.
